# ResetPoint

**Know your biases. Trade with clarity.**

ResetPoint is a behavioral finance analytics tool that analyzes your trading data to detect cognitive and emotional biases such as overtrading, revenge trading, loss aversion, and recency bias. It then provides clear, actionable feedback to improve trading discipline.

---

## What It Does

- Upload a CSV of your trades (timestamp, side, quantity, P&L, balance, etc.)
- Generate a psychological trading profile with a bias radar showing detected biases
- Receive three AI-generated strategy adjustments tailored to your results
- Listen to coaching advice and answers via text-to-speech
- Ask the coach preset questions (e.g. *“What is revenge trading?”*, *“What did my analysis find?”*) and receive spoken responses
- Drill down into evidence for each detected bias (specific trades and timestamps) in the **Evidence Locker**
